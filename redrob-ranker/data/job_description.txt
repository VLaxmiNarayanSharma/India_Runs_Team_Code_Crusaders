Senior Machine Learning Engineer — Search, Retrieval & Ranking
Redrob AI · Bangalore, India (Hybrid)

About the role
Redrob is building India's AI-native hiring platform. We need a senior ML engineer to own the intelligence layer that matches candidates to roles — going far beyond keyword filters. You will design, ship, and iterate on hybrid retrieval, embedding pipelines, learning-to-rank models, and evaluation harnesses used in production at scale.

What you will do
- Build and productionize hybrid retrieval systems combining lexical search (BM25) with dense vector recall across millions of profiles.
- Select, fine-tune, and evaluate embedding models for candidate–JD matching.
- Design learning-to-rank and recommendation features using behavioral signals (clicks, saves, recruiter actions).
- Own offline/online evaluation: NDCG, MRR, recall@K calibrated to live A/B metrics.
- Ship end-to-end ML systems with strict latency budgets (sub-200ms p95) and high QPS.
- Partner with product, data engineering, and recruiting ops in a fast-moving startup environment.

Must-have qualifications
- 5–10 years of hands-on experience building production ML systems (not research-only).
- Strong track record in search, information retrieval, recommendation systems, or ranking at scale.
- Deep expertise in embeddings, vector databases, and hybrid retrieval architectures.
- Experience with learning-to-rank (XGBoost, LightGBM, or neural rankers) in production.
- Proficiency in Python and modern ML tooling (PyTorch, scikit-learn, MLflow or similar).
- Rigorous approach to evaluation — you know when offline metrics lie.
- Demonstrated ownership: you have shipped meaningful systems, not just prototypes.

Nice to have
- LLM/RAG experience for semantic matching and query understanding.
- Experience in hiring tech, marketplaces, or two-sided platforms.
- Familiarity with Milvus, FAISS, Elasticsearch, or similar retrieval infrastructure.
- Open-source contributions or strong GitHub activity in ML/IR.

Soft skills we value
- Bias toward shipping: working v1 in 6 weeks beats perfect v2 in 6 months.
- Cross-functional communication with PMs and engineers.
- Pragmatic tradeoffs between classical IR and modern LLM approaches.
- End-to-end ownership from problem framing through deployment and monitoring.

Logistics
- Location: Bangalore, Karnataka, India (candidates willing to relocate or work hybrid preferred).
- Work mode: Hybrid (3 days in office).
- Notice period: ideally ≤ 60 days.
- Expected experience band: 5–10 years.
